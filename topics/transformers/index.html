Fundamental of Transformers & LLMs

GPT, Claude, Gemini, Llama — they're all the same architecture scaled up. A transformer is "attention + feed-forward, stacked." Once you see one block, you've seen the whole thing — every large language model is just more of them, fed more data.

Pairs with Machine Learning — gradient descent and neural networks from that topic are the foundation under everything here.
