Lesson 07

Demand Response

Instead of building peakers to cover a 100-hour-a-year spike, pay big consumers to shed load. Aluminum smelters, cement kilns, data centers, EV fleets, and even water heaters can all flex. Demand response is the cheapest 'plant' on the system.

Try it

Configure a heat-wave hour and dispatch DR vs peakers — see what each costs.

Key takeaways

  • DR competes with peakers — and wins on cost in most ISO capacity auctions.
  • Industrial DR: smelters can drop 200 MW with one phone call.
  • Residential DR: smart thermostats, EV charging shifted by an hour.
  • Total US DR capacity is now ~30 GW — bigger than the entire nuclear fleet of South Korea.
