Storage Arbitrage

A grid-scale battery charges when wholesale prices are low (overnight, midday solar) and discharges when they peak (evening, heat wave). Profit per cycle = (sell price − buy price) × MWh × round-trip efficiency, minus the cycle cost.

Try it

Tune the price spread and battery size, see the daily and annual revenue.

Key takeaways

  • Round-trip efficiency: lithium ~88%, pumped hydro ~80%, hydrogen ~40%.
  • Daily price spread of $50/MWh is enough for most lithium projects to pencil.
  • Cycle life sets the daily revenue cap — a 4000-cycle pack lasts ~11 years at one cycle/day.
  • Longer-duration storage (8h+) needs lower-cost chemistry — flow, iron-air, thermal.
